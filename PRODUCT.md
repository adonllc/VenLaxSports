# Product

## Register

product

## Users

USA recreational-to-competitive sports players — Tennis and Pickleball (Phase 1), Cricket added in Phase 2. Players range from 2.5-level hobbyists to serious 4.5+ competitors. Primary context: mobile, on or near a court, checking between matches or sessions. They want organized competition without needing to manage it themselves.

## Product Purpose

VENLAX Sports organizes ranked amateur sports leagues across US cities. Players discover leagues in their city and sport, join (free or paid via Stripe), report match scores, track their ELO-style rating across seasons, and advance through playoffs. The platform makes league infrastructure — scheduling, standings, bracket generation, payments, notifications — invisible so the sport is all that remains.

Primary task on PlayerDashboard: discover and join new leagues. Acquisition is a first-class experience, not an afterthought.

## Brand Personality

Premium-athletic. Clean, confident, serious craft — less hype, more authority. Think Strava or Whoop, not Nike training ads. The copy says "Dominate" but the emotional reality for the player is: *I know where I stand. I know what's next.*

Three words: Precise. Competitive. Purposeful.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS** (Intercom, HubSpot style): blue gradients, stock photography, bullet-point benefit heroes, rounded-everything friendliness
- **Fitness influencer apps**: aggressive motivational overlays, over-animation, dark mode as default aesthetic, Instagram-gradient energy

## Design Principles

1. **Precision over hype** — show real data (rating, rank, standings, next match). Earn trust with accuracy. Motivational copy is noise; competitive truth is the product.
2. **Sport color as primary identity** — Tennis emerald (#10B981), Pickleball orange (#F97316), Cricket blue (#2563EB) are load-bearing. Every sport-scoped surface should feel unmistakably that sport's color. Never dilute or neutralize these.
3. **Mobile-in-mind, desktop-respected** — players check on courts between sets. Dense information must breathe. Touch targets large, hierarchy immediate, no horizontal scroll.
4. **Competitive clarity through hierarchy** — what changed (rank, score, league status) surfaces first. History and details are one level deep, never buried.
5. **Discovery as first-class** — joining a league should feel as effortless as browsing. The acquisition loop (find → preview → join) must never require more than three taps.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Sport colors must pass 4.5:1 contrast against white backgrounds when used for text; use as backgrounds only with appropriate foreground. Reduced-motion support for all animated elements (schedule media queries). Keyboard navigation for all interactive surfaces.
